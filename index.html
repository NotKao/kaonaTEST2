#3: Boy Scout (31 July - 1 August 2025)

We learn a lot in 2 days because of Boy Scout!
On our first camp, We learned about how to tie a rope, and we passed the Rope Camp!
Our second camp was about using a SILVA Compass. One of my favorite Boy Scout camp in 2025. We didn't passed but at least we got a knowledge.
Our third camp was tenting. I've got the job to hold the stick on the right of the tent. We passed btw :D
Our fourth camp was pretty strict because it's about Boy Scout's command.
Our fifth camp was divided into 3 sub-camps. Common Accidents, Internal Medicine, and External Medicine.
Our sixth camp was about memorizing 10 rules & 3 vows of Boy Scout.
And finally, Our seventh camp was about planning what to pack on Boy Scout's adventure.
We've got 7 Boy Scout knowledges in 2 days!

#4: Brushing Project (4 August 2025)

Which whoever brushes their teeth and don't have any dirty teeth will get a chance to get a reward, Which is for 10 people only.
Before that day comes. I send a brushing tutorial into our class Line Group.
And I even practice for the project.
My result is ... I had 3 dirty teeth. Which it's okay at least we get a toothpaste for free! :D

#7: Morality Camp (19 August 2025)

Morality Camp was VERY VERY fabulous! One of my most favorite and most memorable Mathayom 1 Term 1 activities in CRMS6. There's a lot to tell that I can even talk for hours!
At the morning. We get to do some random activities. There's a several activities to do before we started our first camp. Including Bee in a Hive, Just Dance - Y.M.C.A, Math Defusion, etc.
After that, We begin our first camp. Which is ความพอเพียง (Khwam Pho Phieng) [Sufficiency] camp. There's not something much to tell but we get to wrote 10 things we want and put it into the drawings.
And finally! Our sixth camp was a duck??? Yeah! That's right because the camp is named "เป็ดชิงพื้นที่" (Ped ching phunthi). Which there will be a duck who will steal an empty seat. And someone must sacrifice their seat for duck to come to their seat.
My favorite camp in Morality Camp is our final camp!
